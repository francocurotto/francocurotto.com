🧇 Waffles in Sandwich Maker

📋 Ingredients

  • 🍞 100 g all-purpose flour with baking powder
  • 🧂 25 g granulated sugar
  • 🧂 1/4 tsp salt
  • 🥛 125 ml milk (whole or semi-skimmed)
  • 🧈 30 g unsalted butter, melted
  • 🥚 1 large egg
  • 🥄 1 tsp vanilla extract

🔢 Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients in Thermomix.
  2. Put mix in mold of sandwich maker and cook for 8-10 minutes.
